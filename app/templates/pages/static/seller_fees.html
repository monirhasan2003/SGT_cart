    SGT Cart's pricing is simple: a flat platform commission per delivered sale, no monthly subscription, no listing fee. Optional promotional services are billed separately and only when you opt in. This page lists every fee with the exact rate.

    1. Platform commission

    Commission is charged only on delivered Sub-Orders. Cancelled or refunded Sub-Orders incur no commission. The rate depends on category:

    CategoryCommission rate
    Default (most categories)10.0%
    Electronics & mobiles8.0%
    Books & stationery5.0%
    Fashion (clothing, shoes, accessories)12.0%
    Health & beauty12.0%
    Home & living10.0%
    Grocery (FMCG)6.0%
    Watches & jewellery8.0%
    Toys & baby10.0%

    Commission is calculated on the item subtotal (units × unit price after any flash-sale discount and bulk tier), before VAT. Admin may negotiate a custom rate with high-volume Sellers; the rate for your shop is always visible in the Seller Center.

    2. Payment-processing fees

    Payment-network fees are passed through at cost — SGT Cart doesn't mark them up:

    MethodFee (deducted from Seller's earning)
    SSLCommerz card (Visa / MasterCard / AMEX)2.5% of order amount
    bKash1.85% of order amount
    Nagad1.5% of order amount
    Cash on DeliveryTk 20 flat per Sub-Order (covers courier cash handling)
    SGT Cart Wallet credit / Reward Points0% — no external network involved

    These are the rates SGT Cart pays our payment partners; they're shown on every payout statement so you can reconcile.

    4. Refunded-order commission reversal

    When a delivered Sub-Order is refunded later (return, dispute, Buyer Protection), SGT Cart's commission is refunded too in proportion to the refund. Specifically:

    • Full refund → 100% commission reversed.
    • Partial refund (e.g., 30%) → 30% of commission reversed.
    • The reversal appears as a negative commission line on your next payout statement.

    Payment-processing fees are NOT reversed (the gateway charged us regardless of refund) — they remain a cost of the original sale.

    5. VAT collection & deposit

    Under NBR rules for online marketplaces, SGT Cart withholds VAT at source on certain category sales (currently 5% for most categories) and deposits it to NBR. You'll see:

    • The VAT amount deducted from your gross sale on each payout statement.
    • A monthly VAT certificate suitable for your own VAT return filing (Mushok 6.3).
    • An annual Form 108 / 108B equivalent for income-tax filing.

    If your business is below the BIN/VAT threshold, you should mark this in your Seller profile and SGT Cart will not withhold VAT — but you remain responsible for any tax obligation that arises.

    6. Worked example — a Tk 1,000 sale

    Customer pays Tk 1,000 (incl. VAT) for a fashion item, by bKash.

    Gross order amountTk 1,000.00
    Item subtotal (assuming 5% VAT-inclusive price)Tk 952.38
    VAT portion (5%)Tk 47.62
    Commission @ 12% on item subtotal− Tk 114.29
    bKash gateway fee @ 1.85% on Tk 1,000− Tk 18.50
    VAT withheld & remitted to NBR− Tk 47.62
    Net to your WalletTk 819.59

    Same Order via COD instead of bKash would deduct Tk 20 flat instead of Tk 18.50 — almost identical for this size. Card-paid Orders deduct 2.5% instead of 1.85%, which is Tk 6.50 more for a Tk 1,000 sale.

    Your monthly payout statement breaks this down per Sub-Order so you can reconcile with your accounting.
