    4. SGT Cart's marketplace role

    SGT Cart is an online marketplace — a venue that connects independent Sellers with Customers. For every Listing:

    • The Seller is the contracting party who actually sells the product to you.
    • SGT Cart provides the technology, payment processing, dispute mediation, and delivery coordination — but does not own the inventory listed by Sellers (unless a Listing is expressly marked "Sold & Shipped by SGT Cart").
    • The legal contract for the sale of goods is formed between you and the Seller. SGT Cart is not a party to that contract of sale, except where it acts as Seller of record.

    Despite this, SGT Cart commits to the customer protections described in Buyer Protection, Refund Policy and Dispute Resolution — so you have a clear path to remedy if a Seller fails to deliver, delivers the wrong item, or sells a counterfeit.

    5. Listings & accuracy

    Sellers are responsible for the accuracy of every Listing, including title, description, photographs, specifications, price, weight, variant options and stock quantity. SGT Cart screens Listings for prohibited items, counterfeits and policy violations, but does not warrant that every Listing is error-free or that the product depicted is identical in every detail to the item shipped.

    If you receive a product that materially differs from its Listing, you may seek a refund or replacement under §10 and the Refund Policy.

    6. Pricing & currency

    All prices on the Platform are shown in Bangladeshi Taka (BDT, Tk) and are inclusive of VAT where applicable. Sellers are required to display VAT-inclusive pricing under NBR rules.

    Shipping fees, optional add-ons and Cash-on-Delivery surcharges (if any) are shown separately at checkout before you confirm the Order. Promotional discounts, coupon savings, reward-point redemptions and bulk-pricing tiers are likewise displayed before payment.

    If a Listing displays a price that is clearly a mistake (for example, a Tk 50,000 product mistakenly listed at Tk 50), SGT Cart and the Seller reserve the right to cancel the affected Sub-Order and refund in full. We will notify you and never charge a price you did not confirm at checkout.

    7. Placing an order

    When you place an Order:

    1. You make an offer to buy the items in your cart from the respective Sellers, on the terms shown at checkout.
    2. SGT Cart sends you an order confirmation email and an in-app notification with a unique Order number.
    3. An Order is accepted by a Seller only when that Seller marks the Sub-Order as "processing". Until then the Seller may decline (typically for out-of-stock, address-unreachable, or payment-not-cleared reasons) and you will be refunded in full.

    Multi-vendor Orders are split into Sub-Orders, one per Seller. Each Sub-Order is fulfilled and may be cancelled or refunded independently.

    8. Payment

    The Platform supports the following payment methods (full details at Payment Methods):

    • Cash on Delivery (COD) — pay the delivery agent in cash on receipt.
    • bKash & Nagad — via mobile-wallet redirection.
    • Card (Visa, MasterCard, AMEX, Discover) — via SSLCommerz, a PCI-DSS-compliant gateway. SGT Cart never sees or stores your full card number.
    • Reward Points — redeem your balance at checkout up to the cap displayed.

    By submitting payment information you authorise SGT Cart and our payment processor to charge the total displayed at checkout. Failed prepaid payments leave the Order in an unpaid state — you can retry from your My Orders page.

    9. Delivery & risk of loss

    Estimated delivery times are shown on each product page and at checkout, based on the destination district and the Seller's processing time (typically 1-7 business days within Bangladesh). Delivery time may vary during national holidays, force-majeure events, and severe weather.

    Risk of loss transfers to you upon successful delivery to the address you provided. If delivery fails because nobody was available, the carrier will reattempt up to twice; thereafter the Sub-Order may be cancelled and refunded (less actual return-shipping cost where applicable). Full details: Shipping & Delivery.

    10. Returns & refunds

    You may return most products within 7 days of delivery if they are unused, in original packaging, and with all tags. Some categories are non-returnable for health or hygiene reasons (perishables, intimate wear, custom-made goods) — these are clearly marked on the product page.

    Damaged-on-arrival, wrong-item, or counterfeit deliveries are eligible for a full refund or replacement at no return-shipping cost to you, regardless of category. The full procedure, timelines and refund methods live in the Refund Policy and Returns pages.

    11. Rewards & promotions

    Reward Points are credited to your account when a Sub-Order is marked delivered, at a rate disclosed in Rewards Program. Points are non-transferable and have no cash-out value; they can only be redeemed at checkout on subsequent Orders.

    Coupons, flash sales, sponsored placements and "free-shipping threshold" promotions are governed by their individual terms shown at the point of use. Promotions are non-stackable unless explicitly allowed. SGT Cart reserves the right to revoke promotional benefits gained through abuse (account farming, voucher resale, click fraud).
