SGT Cart (legal name: Smart Global Trade Cart) is a Bangladesh-first multi-vendor marketplace that connects customers with verified local sellers — fashion, electronics, home, beauty, groceries and more, all in one place at sgtcart.com.

Our mission

Make selling online accessible to every small shop in Bangladesh, and give customers a trustworthy place to discover and buy from them — with bilingual support, fast delivery, and Cash-on-Delivery from day one.

What sets us apart
  • Bilingual — every page works in Bangla and English.
  • Verified sellers — each shop submits trade-licence + NID before going live.
  • Fair ranking — search results lead with the highest-rated sellers, not paid placements.
  • Flexible payment — Cash on Delivery, bKash, Nagad and card via SSLCommerz.
  • Real reviews — only customers who actually received the product can review it.
